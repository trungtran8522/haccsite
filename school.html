Hybrid-learning

Phoenix Coding Academy is hopeful to return to in-person learning with a target return for students the week of March 30, 2021. Parents may choose to either have their students continue in Virtual Classes for the remainder of the school year or transition to In-Person On-Campus classes. We will continue to monitor the COVID-19 metrics of our local community closely and inform you immediately of any changes to this plan.


Schedule

⦁ March 15- Safety Week: We will utilize the week to bring back staff who have not already returned to campus and finalize our safety procedures. Students follow schedule for virtual/online classes.

⦁ March 22- Welcome Back Week: Each day, we will welcome back a different grade level of students.

⦁ March 30: Launch Week: Based on the numbers of students who will be at school, we will implement an alternative schedule to limit our class sizes. All students for in-person learning will be assigned to a Blue or Silver schedule.
